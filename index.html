24–28 August 2026 · Okayama, Japan

ISTDAS

Intelligent, Sovereign & Trustworthy Data, AI and Services

A small working meeting on smart services, sovereign and edge AI, and coordination in the age of autonomous agents.

Hosted by Okayama University

About the meeting

A small group of researchers is gathering in Okayama to think carefully, together, about how intelligent services, sovereign and edge AI, and multi-agent coordination should evolve over the next five years. We're aiming for less a conference and more a working session — a short, honest list of hard problems, and the start of a collaboration built to last.

Themes we're here to work on

Theme I

Intelligent Smart Services & Agents

What does "smart" really mean for a service or agent today — and where does that intelligence still fall short?

Theme II

Sovereign AI — the Edge Computing Path

Edge computing as one route to data and AI sovereignty, and the trade-offs — privacy, performance, and beyond — it brings.

Theme III

Coordination in the Era of AI

Goals, models, and protocols for coordinating AI-intensive services under uncertainty and shifting service-level agreements.
