A small group of researchers is gathering in Okayama to think carefully, together, about how intelligent services, sovereign and edge AI, and multi-agent coordination should evolve over the next five years. We're aiming for less a conference and more a working session — a short, honest list of hard problems, and the start of a collaboration built to last.
